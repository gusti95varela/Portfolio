Imagining a design can be easy. Committing time and following
through can be difficult. But not impossible

I started designing a new draft of my 2022 portfolio. After sitting for hours and trying to find the design that made me feel comfortable and looked pretty, it took a lot of work but I didn't rest my fingers.

It took me a long time to finish it, but in the end I won. I learned a lot, especially how to deal with the frustration of things not going the way you think and want them to. I realized that doing, deleting, redoing, redoing, redoing, deleting and redoing the code again is a divine experience, that's how I realized that you learn.

Not that I'm complaining. It's practically become a hobby of mine and an obligation to finish this portfolio. In a way, I'm both excited and a little sad that the images / logos / design / coding are finally finished. I was inspired by a "childlike and animated" design using HTML, CSS, Animations (@Key Frames), Responsive Design (Mobile, Tablet, Desktop).
