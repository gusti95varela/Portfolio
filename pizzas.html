Pizza's And Roll

It is a mobile application whose objective is to help you order pizza quickly and comfortably.

In my early days of freelancing as a UX/UI designer I had the opportunity to provide services for a client who was looking to create a pizza delivery application. I worked on their logo in reference to their name whose slogan was "Today is for a Pizza and a lot of Rock And Roll".
